The Best BoyBand in the world.

BTS, short for Bangtan Sonyeondan or Bulletproof Boy Scouts, is a globally acclaimed South Korean boy band that has taken the music industry by storm. Formed by Big Hit Entertainment, the group consists of seven members: RM, Jin, Suga, J-Hope, Jimin, V, and Jungkook. Debuting in 2013, BTS has since achieved unprecedented success, becoming a cultural phenomenon with a massive and dedicated fanbase known as the "ARMY." Renowned for their dynamic performances, meaningful lyrics, and genre-defying music, BTS has transcended language barriers, making a significant impact on the global music scene. Their discography encompasses a diverse range of genres, from hip-hop to pop, and their socially conscious lyrics often address mental health, self-love, and social issues.
